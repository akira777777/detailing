## 2024-05-23 - [Copy Confirmation ID Enhancement]
**Learning:** Providing a one-click copy mechanism for reference numbers (like booking IDs) significantly reduces friction and prevents manual transcription errors. Combining this with immediate toast feedback creates a "delightful" and professional feel.
**Action:** Always consider adding "Click to Copy" functionality to important reference strings (IDs, coupon codes, addresses) in the UI, especially on success/confirmation pages.
